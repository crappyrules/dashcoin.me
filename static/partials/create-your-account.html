Take Note of your Private Login Key!
Below this you will find your thirteen word "Private Login Key". Keeping this secure and private is very important, as it is the only way that you will be able to login to your {{config.platformName}} account. As we don't store your private login key on the server there is no way to recover it if it is lost!

Your private login key can also never be changed, and if it is stolen or otherwise compromised, you will have to move your funds to a new account with a new private login key. Therefore, it is best that you backup your private login key by writing it down, perhaps obscuring it as part of a poem or letter, and storing it in multiple safe and secure places.
Take Note of your View Key!
It is important to understand everyone, who has your address has your view key and is able to track your funds! This is direct consequence of providing you a way to backup both your keys. Always treat web wallets as compromised in terms of viewing and sharing your activity with 3rd parties!
Understand the Risks in Using {{config.platformName}}
{{config.platformName}} is a web-based interface that allows you to use {{config.coinName}} without running a full {{config.coinName}} node. However, because this convenience comes at a cost: it is extremely difficult for {{config.platformName}} to securely deliver its code to your browser. This means that there is considerable risk in using {{config.platformName}} for large amounts!

It is recommended that you treat {{config.platformName}} as you would treat your actual wallet, and not store very large amounts in it. For long-term storage of {{config.coinName}} you should create a cold wallet.
